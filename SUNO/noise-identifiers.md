2. The "Hey!" Vox Protocol
To get the rhythmic "Hey" vocal hit (Vox Hit) in your tracks:
In the Style Box:
 * Rhythmic 'Hey' Vox
 * Syncopated Vocal Chants
 * Off-beat Gang Vocals
In the Lyrics (Suno Cues):
Place these on the off-beats (the 2 and the 4) to create a "thugging" bounce:
[Verse 1]
Locked in the pocket, I'm never gon' stray [Hey!]
Scary Terry coming, get out of the way [Hey!]
[Rhythmic Chants: Hey! Hey! Hey!]

4. Defining "Bubblegum Bass"
Bubblegum Bass is the "plastic" foundation of Hyperpop. It is essential for the "bright poppy" sound you requested.
 * Vibe: Sweet melodies mixed with aggressive, distorted bass.
 * Key Sounds: "Latex" textures, rubbery snaps, and high-pitched vocal chops.
 * Why for you: It is minimalist and clean, making it the perfect backdrop for a high-speed Pocket Flow.
5. Pro-Tips for "Pocket Flow" Cadences
To ensure the AI doesn't drown out your lyrics with a "wall of sound":
 * Use "Staccato": This forces the instruments to be short and punchy.
 * Use "Percussive Silence": A keyword that tells the AI to leave "holes" in the beat.
 * Use "Minimalist Trap Bounce": Keeps the drums simple so your flow is the focus.
